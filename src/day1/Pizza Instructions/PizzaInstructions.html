Pizza Instructions

We are going to make a pizza!

  1. Create a new sketch in Processings and add setup() and draw() methods.

  1. In your setup() method, set the size of your canvas:

                size(int width, int height);

  1. In your setup() method, draw a dough colored ellipse for your pizza, a red ellipse for your sauce, and a yellow ellipse your cheese.

  1. Visit http://beej.us/pizza/pizza.menu.html and find toppings for your pizza

  1. Drag the images onto your desktop, and then drag them into your sketch.

  1. In your draw() method, load the images of your toppings:

                PImage pepperoni = loadImage("pepperoni.gif");        

                image(pepperoni, int xPosition, int yPosition);

  1. Before your image command, resize your toppings:

                pepperoni.resize(10, 10);

  1. Add more toppings:

                image(toppingName, int xPosition, int yPosition);

  1. Add toppings wherever the user clicks the mouse.

        Hint:  Use the mousePressed, mouseX, and mouseY variables

  1. In your setup() method, use a background image to put the pizza in a box:

        PImage pizzaBox = loadImage("box.jpg");        //in setup method

pizzaBox.resize(width, height);        

background(pizzaBox);
