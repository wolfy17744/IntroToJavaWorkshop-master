Zombie Eyes

We’re going to make a face with color-changing eyes!

  1. Create a new sketch in Processing and add setup() and draw() methods.

  1. Find an image of a face on google.  It could be a zombie, a cat, or whatever you like.

  1. Drop your image into your Processing sketch.

  1. Load your image in your setup() method using the following code:

                PImage face = loadImage(“face.jpg”);

                image(face, 0, 0);

  1. Change the code you just entered so that “face.jpg” is the same as the name of your image.  

  1. Set the size of your sketch and the size of your image to be the same.  Enter the following code in between the previous two you entered:

                size(800, 600);

                face.resize(width, height);

  1. In your draw() method, place two ellipses over the eyes.  

  1. Give the irises a color using fill(int red, int green, int blue).

  1. Change your fill command so that the color of the eyes changes when you move the mouse.

        Hint: use mouseX and mouseY.

  1. Draw black pupils on the irises.
