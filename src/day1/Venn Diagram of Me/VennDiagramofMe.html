Venn Diagram of Me

We are going to make a Venn diagram that shows different parts of your personality!

  1. Create a new sketch in Processing and add setup() and draw() methods.  

  1. Add three overlapping circles to your sketch.

  1. Give the circle nice colors using:

                fill(int red, int green, int blue, int transparency);

  1. Label each circle with a part of your personality using:

                text(“message”, int xPosition, int yPosition);
